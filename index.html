Potential Risks and Side Effects

While codeine phosphate is effective for pain and cough management, it carries a risk of side effects and potential for misuse. Understanding these risks is crucial for safe use.

    • Signs of Dependency: Increased tolerance, cravings, and using the meditation outside of prescribed guidelines are warning signs of dependency.

    • Risk Factors: Patients with underlying respiratory conditions, such as asthma or chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), are at higher risk.
    • Emergency Response: Recognizing the signs of respiratory depression, such as blue lips, difficulty breathing, or extreme drowsiness, is crucial for timely intervention.

Symptoms can include rash, itching, swelling, severe dizziness, and difficulty breathing.
